### Course Overview / 课程概述
This course provides comprehensive teaching for AP Calculus AB and BC, following the official College Board curriculum framework. It focuses on systematic learning of calculus concepts and rigorous exam-oriented training for international high school students.
本课程针对AP微积分AB与BC提供系统化教学，严格遵循College Board官方考纲，面向国际高中学生开展完整的微积分知识学习与标准化备考训练。

### Course Content / 课程内容
Covers all key topics of AP Calculus AB & BC: Limits & Continuity, Derivatives, Applications of Differentiation, Integrals, Applications of Integration, Differential Equations, Sequences & Series (BC only), and Parametric/Polar Functions (BC only).
全面覆盖AP微积分AB/BC全部核心内容：极限与连续性、导数、微分应用、积分、积分应用、微分方程、级数（BC专属）、参数方程与极坐标（BC专属）。

### Teaching Focus / 教学重点
Focus on logical understanding of calculus principles, standard problem-solving methods, exam question analysis, and step-by-step calculation skills. Help students grasp key scoring points and efficiently improve exam performance.
侧重微积分原理的逻辑理解、标准化解题方法、真题题型分析与规范步骤训练，帮助学生快速掌握考试核心得分点，高效提升应试能力。

### Learning Goals / 学习目标
Help students fully master the knowledge and skills of AP Calculus AB/BC, develop mathematical reasoning and modeling abilities, and achieve high scores in the official AP exam to obtain university credits.
帮助学生全面掌握AP微积分AB/BC知识体系，培养数学推理与建模能力，在AP官方考试中取得高分并获得大学学分认可。
