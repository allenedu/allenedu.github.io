### Course Overview / 课程概述
This course provides comprehensive teaching for AP Calculus AB and BC, following the official College Board curriculum framework. It focuses on systematic learning of calculus concepts and rigorous exam-oriented training for international high school students.

本课程针对AP微积分AB与BC提供系统化教学，严格遵循College Board官方考纲，面向国际高中学生开展完整的微积分知识学习与标准化备考训练。

### Course Content / 课程内容
Covers all key topics of AP Calculus AB & BC: Limits & Continuity, Derivatives, Applications of Differentiation, Integrals, Applications of Integration, Differential Equations, Sequences & Series (BC only), and Parametric/Polar Functions (BC only).

全面覆盖AP微积分AB/BC全部核心内容：极限与连续性、导数、微分应用、积分、积分应用、微分方程、级数（BC专属）、参数方程与极坐标（BC专属）。

### Teaching Focus / 教学重点
Focus on logical understanding of calculus principles, standard problem-solving methods, exam question analysis, and step-by-step calculation skills. Help students grasp key scoring points and efficiently improve exam performance.

侧重微积分原理的逻辑理解、标准化解题方法、真题题型分析与规范步骤训练，帮助学生快速掌握考试核心得分点，高效提升应试能力。

### Learning Goals / 学习目标
Help students fully master the knowledge and skills of AP Calculus AB/BC, develop mathematical reasoning and modeling abilities, and achieve high scores in the official AP exam to obtain university credits.

帮助学生全面掌握AP微积分AB/BC知识体系，培养数学推理与建模能力，在AP官方考试中取得高分并获得大学学分认可。


### Course Outline / 课程大纲

**2. Limits & Continuity / 极限与连续性**

2.1 Concept & Formal Definition of Limits / 极限的概念与严格定义

2.2 Limit Operation Laws & Calculation Rules / 极限运算定理与求解法则

2.3 Two Classic Important Limits / 两大重要极限及其应用

2.4 Function Asymptotes & End Behavior Analysis / 函数渐近线与趋势分析

2.5 Continuity & Discontinuity of Functions / 函数的连续与间断性

2.6 Continuity Theorems & Practical Applications / 连续性相关定理与应用

**3. Derivative Concept & Calculation / 导数概念与计算**

3.1 Definition of Derivative & Difference Quotient / 导数定义与差商公式

3.2 One-Sided Derivatives & Differentiability Judgment / 单侧导数与可导性判定

3.3 Geometric Meaning of Derivatives / 导数的几何意义与切线问题

3.4 Basic & Advanced Differentiation Formulas / 基础及高阶求导公式

3.5 Implicit Differentiation & Inverse Function Differentiation / 隐函数与反函数求导

3.6 Derivatives of Special Functions (BC only) / 特殊函数求导（参数、极坐标、向量函数）（BC专属）

**4. Applications of Derivatives / 导数综合应用**

4.1 Tangent & Normal Line Equations / 切线与法线方程求解

4.2 Differential Mean Value Theorems / 微分中值定理及应用

4.3 Related Rates & Dynamic Change Problems / 相关变化率问题

4.4 Particle Motion & Kinematics Analysis / 质点运动学分析

4.5 Function Monotonicity, Extremum &最值 / 函数单调性、极值与最值

4.6 L'Hôpital's Rule for Indeterminate Limits / 洛必达法则求解未定式极限

**5. Differentials & Numerical Approximation / 微分与数值近似**

5.1 Concept & Properties of Differentials / 微分的概念与基本性质

5.2 Linear Approximation of Functions / 函数线性近似估算

5.3 Euler's Method (BC only) / 欧拉数值解法（BC专属）

**6. Indefinite Integrals & Integration Techniques / 不定积分与积分方法**

6.2 Basic Integration Formulas & Operations / 基础积分公式与运算

6.3 U-Substitution Integration Method / 换元积分法

6.4 Integration by Parts (BC only) / 分部积分法（BC专属）

6.5 Partial Fraction Integration (BC only) / 分式拆分积分法（BC专属）

**7. Definite Integrals & Core Theorems / 定积分与核心定理**

7.1 Riemann Sum & Definite Integral Definition / 黎曼和与定积分定义

7.2 Fundamental Theorems of Calculus / 微积分基本定理

7.3 Properties & Operation of Definite Integrals / 定积分性质与运算

**8. Applications of Integrals / 积分实际应用**

8.1 Integral Mean Value Theorem / 积分中值定理

8.2 Plane Area Calculation / 平面图形面积求解

8.3 Solid Volume Calculation / 立体体积计算

8.4 Curve Arc Length Calculation (BC only) / 曲线弧长计算（BC专属）

**9. Differential Equations / 微分方程**

9.2 Slope Fields & Solution Curve Analysis / 斜率场与解曲线分析

10.2 Common Series Convergence Tests / 常用级数审敛法

10.4 Power Series & Taylor Series Expansion / 幂级数与泰勒级数展开

10.5 Series Approximation & Error Analysis / 级数近似与误差分析
