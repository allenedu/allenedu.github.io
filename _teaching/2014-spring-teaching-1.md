### Course Overview / 课程概述
This course is designed for international high school students, covering the complete official curriculum of AP Precalculus to bridge the gap between high school mathematics and university-level calculus.
本课程面向国际高中学生，完整覆盖AP预微积分官方考纲内容，衔接高中基础数学与大学微积分体系。

### Course Content / 课程内容
Covers all core modules of official AP Precalculus: Functions, Polynomial & Rational Functions, Exponential & Logarithmic Functions, Trigonometric & Polar Functions, Parametric Equations, Vectors and Matrices, and Mathematical Modeling.
全面涵盖AP预微积分核心考点：函数体系、多项式与有理函数、指数与对数函数、三角函数与极坐标函数、参数方程、向量与矩阵、数学建模。

### Teaching Focus / 教学重点
Focus on intuitive understanding of abstract mathematical concepts, standardized exam problem-solving strategies, and logical reasoning training. Effectively help students eliminate knowledge blind spots and adapt to AP exam logic.
侧重抽象概念通俗化讲解、标准化解题思路梳理与逻辑思维训练，帮助学生扫清知识盲区，适配AP考试出题逻辑。

### Learning Goals / 学习目标
Build a complete and rigorous precalculus knowledge system, lay a solid foundation for subsequent AP Calculus learning, and help students steadily achieve high scores in official AP examinations.
搭建完整、严谨的预科数学知识体系，为后续AP微积分学习筑牢基础，助力学生在AP正式考试中稳定出分。
